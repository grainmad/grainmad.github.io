Taylor series

$f(x) = f(x)+f’(x)x+\frac{f’’(x)}{2!}x^2+\frac{f’’’(x)}{3!}x^3+\dots++\frac{f^{(n)}(x)}{n!}x^n = \sum \limits_{k=0}^{n}\frac{f^{(k)}(x)}{k!}x^k$

$f^n(x)$ $f^n(0)$ $a_n=\frac{f^n(0)}{n!}$
